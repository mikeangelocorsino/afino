Credit Information Exchange


In the pursuit of financial excellence, AFINO champions a robust Credit Information Exchange (CIE) platform as a cornerstone of our commitment to responsible lending and comprehensive financial services. This innovative system plays a pivotal role in enhancing credit management, fostering transparency, and facilitating informed decision-making.

Our Credit Information Exchange is designed to promote financial inclusion by providing a standardized platform for sharing credit data. This facilitates greater access to credit for individuals and businesses, particularly those who may not have an extensive credit history. By incorporating alternative data sources, we strive to create a more inclusive credit environment.
